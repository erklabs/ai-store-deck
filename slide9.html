LAUNCH, SCALE & FLYWHEEL PHASES

🚀 Go-To-Market Roadmap (0-36M)

PHASE 1 (0-6 M)

Launch & Product-Market Fit

Targeting early adopters and time-poor Gen-Z / student cohorts to establish our foundational user loops.
  • Day-One Value Launch 3 high-utility 'Hero Agents' (Travel, Finance, Food).
  • Organic Growth TikTok demonstrations, campus ambassador referrals.
  • Freemium Model Free core features, €5/mo compute tier.
PHASE 2 (6-18 M)

Scale & Developer Ecosystem

Releasing external developer tooling to transform a single storefront into a self-sustaining ecosystem.
  • Developer SDK Release secure tunneling and containment APIs to builders.
  • Marketplace Launch Open agent catalog sales with 15% platform take-rate.
  • SME Pilots Launch B2B operational sandboxes for tech-forward offices.
PHASE 3 (18-36 M)

Enterprise SaaS & Flywheel

Expanding operational scale through advanced custom swarms, compliance, and custom workspace panels.
  • Visual Swarm Builder Drag-and-drop tool to orchestrate multi-agent runs.
  • Organic Discovery Semantic recommenders match agents with users.
  • Enterprise Dashboard Central consoles, SSO, custom audit trails.
Our ultimate horizon: Unlocking a self-sustaining agent flywheel that makes autonomous internet action accessible to everyone.
